What is Scriblr?
For Readers
  1. 1.

    Enjoy stories written by the the Scriblr Community for free.

  2. 2.

    Read premium original stories crafted by top-notch writers exclusively for Scriblr Premium Users.

  3. 3.

    Experience fiction like never before through the innovative formats of our Originals including Interactive, Multiplayer, Open World, Augmented Reality Fiction etc.

For Writers
A payment system that values your work and puts the power of payment in the hands of the readers.
  1. 1.

    Post your stories on Scriblr and reach readers all across the world.

  2. 2.

    Join Scriblr as a premium member to be able to join our Writer Program and monetize your work.

  3. 3.

    When a reader joins Scriblr Premium, a portion of their subscription fee directly goes to the stories they like and engage with the most.

  4. 4.

    The more Premium users engage with your stories, the more you'll earn.

  5. 5.

    See the breakdown of your earnings in the revenue section of your profile.

  6. 6.

    Get paid for your reader engagement at the end of every calender month.

  7. 7.

    Write the best stories you can and publicize them to earn more from your work.

About Us
Scriblr is a startup based in India, founded by a group of passionate college students. Our core team consists of writers and developers who understand the millenial generation and how it thinks, and knows what this generation of storytellers needs through personal experience. We've come together to change how you read and write stories. We're based in Pune, although we have volunteers helping out from all over the country. The goal by the time we launch is to have a reach not only across India but across the United States, Europe and other major markets for content and literature consumption.
Careers at Scriblr
