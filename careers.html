Mission

Here at Scriblr we are looking for passionate people who want to change the way people read stories. We don't care where you are in the world, if you're interested in building this company from the ground up, we want to get to know you. We want to create a culture of a hard-working team which communicates and works well together. We're a very young company, so we encourage students who are interested in the idea to apply if they have the necessary skills. We think we have something special here, and we'd love for you to join us if you think you're up for it.

How It'll Work

At Scriblr one of the first things we prioritize is fit. The recruitment process to join Scriblr is two-step.

The first thing we'll do is evaluate your portfolio and resumes and select those we feel have the skillset to excel with our team. We realize that as an early-stage startup, highly skilled individuals will be difficult to recruit so we are targeting young students and graduates who want to find a place to grow and hone their skills by working with like-minded people.

The second step will be a short personal interview over video-call to gauge the personality fit. Scriblr is just starting up, but we want to make sure every member we add to the team fits in.

Intern With Scriblr

We realize how difficult it is for high school and college students to find internships where they will have a meaningful role and feel valued and polish their skills in a professional environment.

Internships in the above categories are available for durations of:

  • 4 weeks
  • 12 weeks
We encourage both local and remote students to apply for these internships where they will work alongside the founders and the core team to solve day to day problems leading up to the launch.

Why Should You Intern With Scriblr?

  • - The Ability to Learn New Skills in an Enviroment with less pressure.
  • - Ability to make a difference and affect decisions made by the founders.
